# Marking Rubric

## Technical Implementation and Architecture (40 points)

| Criterion | Points | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Google Technology Integration | 15 | Meaningful and integral use of at least one Google Developer technology; demonstrates understanding of why the technology was chosen and how it enhances the solution |
| AI Implementation Quality | 10 | AI is essential to the solution (not decorative); appropriate AI model choice with justification; consideration of ethical AI aspects (bias, hallucination mitigation, privacy, transparency) |
| Working Demonstration & UI/UX | 10 | Functional prototype demonstrating core features; stable execution with no critical or fatal bugs; clear and intuitive user experience |
| AI Model Performance | 5 | Accuracy, efficiency, and effectiveness of AI components; evidence of efforts to reduce hallucinations or incorrect outputs |

## Business Innovation and Problem Solving (40 points)

| Criterion | Points | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Originality and Creativity | 10 | Novel or creative approach to addressing the problem; innovative use of AI and/or Google technologies; clear differentiation from existing solutions or approaches |
| Problem-Solution Fit & Real-World Relevance | 15 | Well-defined real-world problem; clear identification of stakeholders and target beneficiaries; solution directly addresses the problem in a practical and realistic way |
| Scalability | 10 | Consideration of scalability, cost, and resource usage; assessment of the proposed business model's viability and potential for sustainable, profitable growth |
| Deployment Readiness | 5 | Feasible deployment approach (cloud, APIs, infrastructure); clear potential to evolve into a real-world product or startup |
