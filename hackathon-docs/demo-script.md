# NEXUS — 3-Minute Pitch Demo Video Script

**Format:** Screen recording with voiceover. No talking head needed.
**Tone:** Confident, technically specific, no filler.
**Total runtime:** 3:00 — paced at ~150 words/minute.

---

## PRE-ROLL (0:00 – 0:05)

*Black screen. Text appears, held for 3 seconds.*

> **"76% of people say mentors are important.**
> **Only 37% have one."**
> *— MentorNeko Research, 2024*

---

## ACT 1: THE PROBLEM (0:05 – 0:25)

**[SCREEN]** Slow pan of a messy spreadsheet — mentor names, startup names, columns of manual notes.

> **VOICEOVER:**
> Innovation programme managers run on spreadsheets and gut feel. They sift through pitch decks manually. They match mentors by instinct. And once the kickoff event ends — relationships die silently, and nobody knows until it's too late.
>
> F6S. Gust. Mentorloop. They all solve one slice.
> None of them treat the relationship itself as the data.
>
> That's the gap. That's what Nexus fixes.

> **VOICEOVER:**
> Nexus is an AI relationship operating system for innovation ecosystems.

## ACT 3: STARTUP ONBOARDING + AI EXTRACTION (0:35 – 1:10)

**[SCREEN]** Click "Continue with Google" → Role select page. Three cards appear — Startup, Mentor, Programme Manager. Click Startup.

> **VOICEOVER:**
> A startup signs in with Google — one click, no passwords.
> They pick their role. Then they build their profile.

**[SCREEN]** Profile setup page. Fill in name, industry, stage. Drag a pitch deck PDF into the upload area. A loading state appears.

> **VOICEOVER:**
> They upload their pitch deck. Gemini 2.0 Flash reads it — not just the text, the whole document, multimodally.
> In about two seconds...

**[SCREEN]** Tags populate: `FinTech`, `MVP`, `B2B SaaS`, `Stripe`. Quality score counts up to 78 out of 100. Four bar segments fill — Problem Clarity, Market Size, Team Strength, MVP Readiness. An AI callout appears below in italic serif.

> **VOICEOVER:**
> ...structured tags extracted. A quality score across four dimensions. And a plain-English AI summary of exactly what the deck does well — and where it needs work.
>
> No human reviewed this deck. No survey. Gemini read it and scored it.

---

## ACT 4: ADMIN GENERATES MATCHES (1:10 – 1:45)

**[SCREEN]** Switch to admin role via the role switcher in the bottom-right corner. Navigate to Admin → Generate Matches. The pool diagram shows startups funnelling into a Gemini node with mentors on the right.

> **VOICEOVER:**
> Switch to the programme manager's view. They see every qualified startup in the pool — only the ones that cleared the quality gate — and every mentor available.
>
> One button.

**[SCREEN]** Click "Generate matches." Loading state. Then: *"23 pairings written."* A table appears — startup, mentor, edge score, and a match narrative excerpt in italic serif.

> **VOICEOVER:**
> Gemini 2.5 Pro reads every startup against every mentor and writes a match narrative for each compatible pair. Not a score. A reason.
>
> *"This mentor led enterprise sales for exactly the stage this startup is entering. The overlap is specific."*
>
> That's not a recommendation system. That's reasoning.

---

## ACT 5: ECOSYSTEM DASHBOARD — THE GRAPH (1:45 – 2:20)

**[SCREEN]** Navigate to Admin → Ecosystem. The Cytoscape graph loads. Mentor nodes in dark ink, startup nodes in pale white. Edges colour-coded: green for healthy, amber for stable, dashed red for decaying.

> **VOICEOVER:**
> The ecosystem dashboard shows the entire cohort as a live relationship graph.
> Green edges — healthy. Amber — stable. Red, dashed — decaying.
>
> A programme manager doesn't need a survey to know a relationship is in trouble. The graph shows it in real time.

**[SCREEN]** Click a dashed red edge. A side panel appears — health score 41, trend: decaying. An AI callout fills the panel with narration text.

> **VOICEOVER:**
> Click the decaying edge. Gemini narrates exactly why.
>
> *"No platform activity in 14 days. Three milestones overdue. Recommend a re-engagement nudge before week eight — or a partial reassignment."*
>
> That narration triggers automatically every time a milestone is completed or missed. No admin had to write it.

**[SCREEN]** Highlight the "Run nudges" button in the topbar.

> **VOICEOVER:**
> One click to act. Before the relationship dies silently.

## Recording Notes

- Record at **1440p minimum** — the graph edges, quality meter bars, and pill tags need to read clearly at full screen.
- Use a **demo account with seeded data**. Cold databases kill demos. You need: a startup profile with a quality score already set, at least 3 relationships at different health levels (one healthy, one stable, one decaying).
- The **role switcher** in the bottom-right corner lets you flip between startup and admin mid-recording without re-logging in. Use it going into Act 4.
- For Act 5, **pre-click the decaying edge once** before recording so Cytoscape has already settled into layout. Then re-click it on camera.
- For Act 3, either pre-upload a real PDF or use the seeded quality score — the AI extraction takes 2–3 seconds live; if it runs long, cut to the result appearing.
- **No background music until the close card.** Let the product speak.
