# PayFlow — Instant Cross-Border Payments for ASEAN SMEs

## The Problem

**SME exporters lose 3-5% on every cross-border payment** due to:
- Multiple intermediary banks (3-5 hops per transaction)
- Hidden FX markups averaging 2.1%
- Settlement times of 3-5 business days
- Lack of transparency in fee structures

**Malaysian SME exporters collectively lost RM 1.2B in payment fees in 2025.**

## Our Solution

**Direct bank-to-bank payment rails** using existing ASEAN banking corridors:
- **< 4 hour settlement** vs 3-5 days industry standard
- **Transparent flat fee** of 0.8% — no hidden markups
- **Real-time tracking** from sender to recipient
- **API-first** integration with existing ERP systems
